# Fanad

A local-first personal scratchpad for tasks, notes, lists, timers and light journaling —
capture by texting a bot (Telegram/Slack) or the built-in web UI. A local LLM (LM Studio or
Ollama, whatever you already run) parses and ranks what you type; it never invents data and
never acts on your house. No nagging, no cloud by default, no telemetry.

Open the **Web UI** after starting, then connect your model in **Settings**. Full setup,
LLM pointing, and backup notes are in the **Documentation** tab.
